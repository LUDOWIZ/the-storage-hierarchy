The Storage Hierarchy 🌍
From Bits to Exabytes: Visualizing the scale, geography, and technology of modern cloud infrastructure.

Ever wondered where your 5 TB Google Drive actually lives? To us, it’s just a number on a screen or a bar filling up. But in reality, it’s a tiny slice of a physical world made of silicon, fiber optics, and massive concrete halls.

This project is a journey from the smallest unit of data to the massive "cloud cities" that store the world's information.

🗺️ What we are mapping
The Micro: How a single bit scales up to the files on your phone.

The Personal: What 5 Terabytes actually looks like (How many miles of disk platters is that?).

The Macro: The geography of Exabyte-scale datacenters. Where are they? Why are they in cold places like Sweden or deserts in Nevada?

✨ The Vision
We want to turn "The Cloud" from an abstract concept into something you can see, touch, and understand. Because the cloud isn't magic—it's geography and physics working at an incredible scale.
